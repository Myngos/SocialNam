Indigenous Media Platform

A community-driven multimedia platform that empowers Indigenous and local communities to create, share, and monetise their cultural content. The platform supports videos, audio, podcasts, and images while prioritising accessibility, cultural sensitivity, and offline functionality.

 Overview

This platform is designed to give communities full control over their digital storytelling and knowledge sharing. It enables creators to distribute content, build audiences, and generate income through subscriptions.
